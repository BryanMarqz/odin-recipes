How to make oat milk:

Oat milk is extremely easy:

Oat milk is beyond easy to make. It has lots of nutritonal values and it's vegan. I recommend you give this recipe a try. It actually takes more time to clean than to actually make it. Just saying, but you should give it a go.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Oats
  • Ice cold Water
  • Optional: Maple Syrup

How to make:

  1. Add oats to blender.
  2. Add ice cold water.
  3. Mix for 30 seconds max
  4. Pour over cloth and strain it.
